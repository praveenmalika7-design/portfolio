Industrial Automation

During my internship at Hemas Manufacturing (Pvt.) Ltd., I contributed to the design, development, implementation, testing, and commissioning of a Semi-Automatic Shampoo Filling Machine intended to replace an existing manual filling process used in commercial production. The project involved mechanical design, electrical system development, industrial automation integration, PLC programming, HMI development, machine assembly, commissioning, and commercial production trials. The machine was developed using Siemens S7-1500 PLC, Siemens HMI, servo motors, servo drives, lobe pumps, pneumatic systems, sensors, and custom mechanical components. The electrical control system was designed using EPLAN software, while the machine structure and assemblies were developed using SolidWorks. A dedicated Clean-In-Place (CIP) system was also integrated to improve machine hygiene, cleaning efficiency, and maintenance accessibility. Multiple production trials were conducted in collaboration with Production, Quality Assurance, Safety, and Research & Development teams to validate machine performance and achieve successful commercial implementation.

Modelling, Development, and Performance Assessment of a Hybrid Powered Thermoelectric Refrigerator

Objective

Deliver a low-cost, portable, and energy-efficient cooling solution for off-grid and rural applications where conventional refrigeration infrastructure is unavailable.

Methodology

Developed a component-level simulation model using MATLAB Simscape & Simulink. Validated model accuracy against prototype measurement data through statistical analysis. Built a virtual testbed to evaluate and optimise design improvements, substantially reducing reliance on repeated physical prototyping.
