Conveyor System Development

Company Hemas Consumer Brands (Pvt.) Ltd., Dankotuwa, Sri Lanka
Duration During Internship — Nov 2025 – May 2026
Project Type Industrial Engineering Project
Status Successfully Commissioned

Designed and developed a 20-foot industrial conveyor system for material handling applications within the production facility. The project encompassed complete mechanical structure design, conveyor belt assembly, electrical control panel construction, Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) motor control configuration, and full system commissioning. The conveyor system enabled controlled, variable-speed material transport, improving production line efficiency and operational safety.

Technologies: Mechanical Design VFD Configuration Electrical Panel Design Motor Control Industrial Automation System Commissioning

Engineering Challenge

The production facility required a reliable, variable-speed material handling solution to transport products between production stages. The challenge was to design a mechanically robust 20-foot conveyor system, construct a safe and compliant electrical control panel, configure a Variable Frequency Drive for controlled motor operation, and commission the complete system to meet production requirements — all within the internship project timeline.

Project Objectives

  • Design and fabricate a 20-foot industrial conveyor system for material handling
  • Construct a fully wired electrical control panel for conveyor operation
  • Configure and integrate a Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) for variable-speed motor control
  • Implement safety features including emergency stop, overload protection, and operational indicators
  • Commission the complete conveyor system on the production floor
  • Validate operational performance through production trials

Engineering Design Process

  1. Requirement Analysis: Defined conveyor length, load capacity, speed requirements, and production floor integration constraints
  2. Mechanical Design: Designed the 20-foot conveyor frame structure, roller arrangement, belt tensioning system, and drive unit mounting
  3. Fabrication & Assembly: Supervised mechanical fabrication and assembly of the conveyor structure, drive unit, and belt system
  4. Electrical Panel Design: Designed the control panel layout including main isolator, MCBs, contactor, VFD, control wiring, and indicator lights
  5. Panel Construction & Wiring: Constructed and fully wired the electrical control panel following industrial wiring standards
  6. VFD Configuration: Programmed VFD parameters including motor nameplate data, acceleration/deceleration ramps, and frequency limits
  7. System Integration: Connected the electrical panel to the conveyor drive motor and integrated all control components
  8. Commissioning & Testing: Conducted no-load and loaded trials, verified speed control, emergency stop response, and overload protection
  9. Handover: Commissioned the system for production use and completed all technical documentation
